About Me

I am a final-year Ph.D student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Irwin King and Michael R. Lyu.

Research Interests

My research interests are in natural language processing and machine learning. My research goal is to enable machines to assist information gathering and knowledge testing through natural language interactions. Recently I work on open-domain question answering, conversational machine reading and question generation.
